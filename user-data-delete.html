Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: November 27, 2025

This app is used only to publish posts to the developer’s own Facebook Page and Instagram Business account via the Meta Graph API from the automation tool n8n.

We do not collect, store, or process any personal user data. The only information used are temporary access tokens provided by Meta for authentication.

If you wish to revoke access or delete any association with this app:

  1. Go to your Facebook Business Integrations settings
  2. Find this app in the list and click “Remove”
  3. Or go to Instagram Accounts Center → Apps and Websites and remove it

Meta automatically deletes expired access tokens. No further action is required.
